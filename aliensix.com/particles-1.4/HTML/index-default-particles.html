Every drop, setting up new billing profiles in-between bots was a pain. Whether it be generating new privacy cards or trying out a new jig that was discovered, re-importing profiles one by one was a tedious process that wasted a lot of valuable time. For that reason, we made a product to make drop setup a breeze!
